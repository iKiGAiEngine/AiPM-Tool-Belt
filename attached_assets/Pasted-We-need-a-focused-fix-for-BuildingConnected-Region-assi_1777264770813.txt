We need a focused fix for BuildingConnected Region assignment in AiPM.

Problem:
Swinerton SoCal BuildingConnected invites are not reliably assigning to the correct internal AiPM LAX subregion.

Important context:
AiPM has Swinerton SoCal broken into these internal Region buckets:

- LAX - FS
- LAX - SPD
- LAX - OCLA
- LAX - TM

The problem is that BuildingConnected may show labels like:

“Swinerton Builders - SoCal - Target Markets”

The current resolver appears to sometimes stop at “SoCal” and assigns plain “LAX” or the wrong LAX bucket. That is wrong because “SoCal” is only the parent clue. The third part of the BC label should determine the internal bucket.

Expected behavior:
- “Swinerton Builders - SoCal - Target Markets” → LAX - TM
- “Swinerton Builders - SoCal - Special Projects” → LAX - SPD
- “Swinerton Builders - SoCal - OCLA” → LAX - OCLA
- “Swinerton Builders - SoCal - Facility Solutions” → LAX - FS
- “Swinerton Builders - SoCal” with no subregion → do not guess. Assign plain LAX or leave blank and require manual review.

Files to inspect first:
- server/autodesk/bcSync.ts
- client/src/pages/ProjectLogPage.tsx
- shared/schema.ts

Do not rewrite the whole sync. Make the smallest safe fix.

1. Find the existing function that resolves/matches regions during BC sync.

   - region id
