How the modules connect
▸Proposal Log → creates the PV Number that every downstream module references
▸Estimating → line items become the buyout budget baseline
▸Buyout → approved vendors feed directly into Submittals
▸Submittals → approved products release Purchase Orders
▸POs + Receiving → 3-way match required before any vendor invoice is paid
▸Billing (SOV) → tracks what NBS bills the GC each month
▸Cash & Closeout → records incoming payments and closes the job
